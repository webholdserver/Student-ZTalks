The Stream's Outline & Flow

We plan to have our stream with 45 minutes as the minimum time and 1 hour as the minimum stream duration. The hosts will ask the intervieweeâ€™s opinion, experiences, and possible answers to the root cause and its effect. We make sure to have an interviewee that is expert or is known in the field that always needs team collaboration for us to have a reliable conversation. For the audiences, we will also hold a raffle with a reward of 50 pesos Gcash in order to increase engagement and interaction.

The Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA)

All of the presented materials in the stream will be coming from the team, no materials from the external sources. However, for the audio, we might use non-copyrighted background music from the internet, but we will still give credits through-out the screen.

Interaction with the Viewers

We will interact with them through shoutouts, reading comments, and the spin the wheel raffle. In a short while, we'll engage in conversation or engagement with the viewers. In order to provide the audience the option to ask us or the interviewee a question about the ineffectiveness of collaborative learning, we will be calling or reading from the crowd in the comments section. For the audiences, we will also hold a raffle with a reward of 50 pesos Gcash in order to increase engagement and interaction. We will ask them what they have learnt in the stream in order to award them the GCash prize, and each audience member who responds in the comment section will be added to the spinning wheel. Then, the name that appears on the wheel's arrow is the winner.
