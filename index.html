THE CONCEPTS OF STREAMING

  • LIVE INTERVIEWS
  • TOOL & PLATFORMS
  • CHARACTERISTICS

Streaming is any form of live or recorded multimedia files that can be broadcasted anytime from platforms to any devices over the internet. One type of stream is LIVE STREAMING, a broadcast that you can watch in real time.

Live Interviews

One of the content types of live streaming is a live interview consisting of at least one host or the interviewer and an interviewee that will simultaneously ask questions, exchange of thoughts and experiences, and talk casually on-the spot and in real time. Weâ€™ll invite people who are experts in the field of our topic beforehand.

In streaming, there are many platforms and tools a streamer or content creator can choose from, based on the preference and needs of your own target audiences or viewers.

Our Stream Tools and Platforms

For our streaming tools, we will maximize and use Open Broadcaster Software or OBS Studio since it is already familiar to our members and they have already tried it. For the platform, we will stream on Facebook & YouTube to attract more viewers and engage with the audiences as these two are few of the most social media applications for our viewers.

Smooth Stream Flow

High or Low-End set of equipment that allows a smooth and laminar flow of the stream and prevents lags, pixelation, and buffering.

Establishes Connection & Flexible

The streamer interacts with the audiences in real-time, with clear audio and video. Streamers should always have a plan whenever unplanned situations occur & unique and different from other streams.

Respectful & Safe Stream Environment

Stream should be influential (positively) and inspiring to call an action to motivate viewers. No toxic and hurtful audience or chat and the streamers behavior, in other words promoting a generally good behavior for both the stream and the audience.

Consistency

Stay consistent with your content uploads, schedule, and your commitment.
