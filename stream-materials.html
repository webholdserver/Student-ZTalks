Student-Z Talks: Promotional Plans and Materials

PROMOTIONAL POSTERS

A promotional poster should consist of a the name and logo, minimal images, attention–grabbing visuals, and a good arrangement of information to make the poster more effective.Important parts of a promotional poster:

  • Headline
  • Information
  • Details
  • Typography
  • Visuals

We are planning to promote the Student-Z Talks stream using these promotional posters by posting it on the Student-Z Talks Page and all of the available social media platforms of the Sixth Sense team. This is because because social media is widely used today and it is the easiest and most efficient way of promoting or advertising something. Here's some samples:

VIDEO AD PROMOTION

A method of advertising certain benefits the audience may gain on our stream through a 22-second video is an act of advertisement of video ads. It consists of typography of the streaming details such as brief information of who we are and the schedule of the stream, along with presentations that indirectly engage viewers why we should join our stream. The goal of promotional video advertisements is to entice visitors to subscribe and watch for our stream.

In promoting using this material, we will be posting this on the Student-Z Talks Facebook Page and YouTube Channel. Aside from that, we will maximize the team members' social media accounts by requiring eveyone to post the video ads on their own accounts.

EMAIL MARKETING & PROMOTION

An email promotion is a marketing strategy to advertise by sending emails to their target customers that may be interested in our stream. It contains the information of what they will expect, the invitational details and what the stream will offer. The purpose of this is to promote our stream so that people would be informed and could check out our channel and page

  • Each member of Sixth Sense team will send promotional emails to the APEC School students’ email address – especially our target viewers, Grade 10 students of APEC Schools Ortigas Extension.

COLLABORATIONAL MARKETING & PROMOTION

A collaborational promotion is a marketing or promotional method where we’ll do alliances with different groups of people and do collaborative marketing initiatives with other brands that have a similar audience. This could allow us to establish a beneficial relationship to help both parties to invite new audiences from promotional ads or videos through social media.

  • In our case, we will be collaborating with other Grade 10 students of APEC Schools Ortigas Extension whereas the other groups will promote our brand name and we will promote theirs too.
